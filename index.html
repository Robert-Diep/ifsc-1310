Thoughts on Web Accessiblility

My thoughts on web accessibility is that developers should make their websites or content in way that is usable for everyone, regardless of their physical or cognitive abilities, or disabilities. We should keep in mind of all persons, making it easier for anyone to access and use the website without any trouble. By keeping these in mind, we can avoid excluding certain people with disabilities. Also ensuring web accessibility from the start can future proof your content, making the website adaptable to changes and technology evolves.
